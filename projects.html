Usable Privacy in Reproductive Health Technologies

I investigate how at-risk populations—particularly women and people with uteruses—experience and navigate privacy risks in reproductive health technologies (e.g., period and fertility tracking (PFT) apps).

Safety by Platform Design in Game Ecosystems

I examine how platform design and governance shape safety and security within game ecosystems where young users participate as both players and creators.
