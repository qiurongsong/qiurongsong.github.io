About me

I’m Qiurong, a second year PhD student studying Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) at Penn State. I’m part of the Health and Play Lab and luckily advised by Dr. Xinning Gui and Dr. Yubo Kou. My research interests are healthcare, privacy, and HCI.
